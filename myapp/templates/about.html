About Us


At Winkel Studio, it’s our firm belief that every individual has the right to look good, feel confident and express themselves through the language of fashion. We take this so seriously, that we have made it our mission to “Create a Better Everyday Lifestyle” for our customers.

We carefully listen to & deeply understand customers’ needs, wants and desires & create accessible fashion brands focused on giving you the trend that you are looking for, in the most efficient and shortest possible time.

We devour the information that consumers share with us and using sophisticated technology, we convert those data insights into fashion products, always fresh and always relevant. Our team of designers, product experts, data analysts and sourcing maestros are constantly working to reach trends from concept to consumer in the shortest possible lead time.

Manufactured In India with the best quality raw material, each product goes through stringent quality checks before its ready to ship, guaranteeing your delight.

Our much loved brand portfolio includes Highlander, Vishudh, Tokyo Talkies, & Locomotive. We got your every fashion need covered from Monday to Sunday & January to December.
